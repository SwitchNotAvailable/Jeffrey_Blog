Woman dies after being hit by car in Co Monaghan

A woman in her 70s has died after being struck by a car in Co Monaghan.



At about 10.45 on Saturday night, a collision occurred on the R183 Newbliss Road near Clonavilla in Clones.

At the site, the pedestrian's death was confirmed. No other injuries were reported, according to Garda. Local detours are in place as forensic collision investigators conduct an examination of the R183 at Clonavilla, which has been shut down. Police are requesting witnesses.


Anybody who was driving on the R183 Newbliss Road between 10 p.m. and 11 p.m. and has video, especially dashcam video, is requested to report it to garda. Anybody with information is urged to call the Garda Confidential Line at 1800 666 111, the Monaghan Garda Station at (047) 77200, or any Garda station.
